Website Planning Document

Renovo Assistance - Specialized Workshop for Renault, Nissan and Multiple Brands

Site Name

Renovo Assistance

Domain: renovoassistance.com

The name "Renovo Assistance" was chosen because it conveys the idea of renewal and assistance, reflecting the company's purpose of offering specialized mechanical services that renew the performance and safety of Renault, Nissan, and other brand vehicles.

Site Purpose

The Renovo Assistance website will serve as a central platform for:

  • Presenting specialized mechanical services for Renault and Nissan vehicles
  • Offering multi-brand services for other manufacturers
  • Providing information about preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Offering an online scheduling channel for services and estimates
  • Providing history of services and maintenance performed
  • Sharing maintenance tips and vehicle care information
  • Presenting the specialized and certified technical team

Scenarios

The following scenarios represent typical questions that website visitors may have:

Scenario 1: Renault owner with specific problem

"My Renault Duster is making a noise in the suspension. Is Renovo specialized in this model and can diagnose it?"

Scenario 2: Nissan owner needing maintenance

"My Nissan Kicks is due for periodic maintenance. Do you work with original parts and have certified technicians?"

Scenario 3: Customer with mechanical emergency

"My Renault Captur's automatic transmission is having problems. Do you perform specific repairs for this transmission?"

Scenario 4: Owner of another brand

"I have a Honda Civic that needs suspension repair. Do you serve multiple brands or only Renault and Nissan?"

Scenario 5: Customer seeking preventive maintenance

"I want to change the brake pads on my Nissan Versa. What's the scheduling timeline and do you use quality parts?"

Color Scheme

The color scheme was selected to convey trust, professionalism, and the idea of assistance and safety:

Primary Orange #f4a837
Secondary Black #040404
Accent Gray #d7d7d7

Color Application:

  • Primary Orange (#f4a837): Header, main buttons, highlight elements, and call-to-action
  • Secondary Black (#040404): Main text, titles, and footer
  • Accent Gray (#d7d7d7): Backgrounds, borders, and secondary elements
